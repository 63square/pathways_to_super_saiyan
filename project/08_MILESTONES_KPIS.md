# 08 — Milestones & KPIs

## 8.1 The Eight Pre-Registered KPIs (Phase 0)

Pre-registered means: the metric, the measurement method, the success threshold, and the statistical test are all locked at Month 0 and cannot be changed without external SAB approval. This prevents post-hoc rationalization.

| KPI | Metric | Method | Baseline (Principal-specific, M0) | Success threshold (M16) | Stat test |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **K1: Cardiorespiratory** | VO₂max (mL/kg/min) | Cosmed K5, ramp protocol on treadmill | Measured M0–M2 | +20% absolute OR top 0.1% age-matched (whichever higher) | Paired pre-post; α=0.01 |
| **K2: Muscular Power** | Peak watts on 6-s sprint | Wattbike Atom | M0–M2 | +25% absolute OR top 0.1% age-matched | Paired pre-post |
| **K3: Muscular Endurance** | TTE at 85% VO₂max | Treadmill, fixed protocol | M0–M2 | +30% absolute | Paired pre-post |
| **K4: Body Composition** | FFM/Total mass ratio | DEXA + ADP | M0–M2 | +5 percentage points absolute | Paired pre-post |
| **K5: Cognitive Performance** | Composite Z-score (n-back, RAVEN, RT) | CANTAB + Cogstate | M0–M2 | Top 0.1% age-matched on composite | Reference population |
| **K6: Biological Age** | DNAm GrimAge | TruDiagnostic | M0–M2 | −3 years vs chronological at M16 | Single timepoint vs reference |
| **K7: Inflammatory & Metabolic** | Composite (hsCRP, fasting insulin, HOMA-IR, IL-6, ApoB) | Standard labs | M0–M2 | All 5 markers in top decile age-matched | Reference population |
| **K8: Recovery Capacity** | HRV recovery slope post-standardized stress | Clinical ECG | M0–M2 | Top 5% age-matched | Reference population |

**Aggregate success:** ≥6 of 8 KPIs hit threshold.

**Tertiary metrics** (~40 more) are measured but not gating. They inform Phase 1 design.

## 8.7 Anti-Cherrypicking Safeguards

To prevent post-hoc rationalization (the most common failure mode of n=1 programs):

1. **All KPIs pre-registered** with thresholds, methods, and statistical tests at Month 0
2. **Statistical analysis plan locked** before unblinding data
3. **External SAB review** at Month 17 before Board decision
4. **Independent third-party labs** for at least 50% of critical biomarkers (TruDiagnostic, Quest, etc.)
5. **Negative results published** internally and considered in spinout pivot decisions
6. **Audit trail** on all data — no editing of raw data, only of analyses, with version control
7. **Pre-committed reasons for failure** documented; if all reasons resolved and KPIs still missed, that means the thesis is wrong, not the execution

The integrity of the program depends on these safeguards. Any C-suite member who attempts to weaken them is grounds for termination.
