# 08 — Milestones & KPIs (v1.1, Post-SAB Review)

## 8.1 The Eleven Pre-Registered KPIs (Phase 0)

Pre-registered at M0 on **public registry** (OSF or equivalent). Metric, measurement method, success threshold, statistical test all locked at M0 and cannot be modified without **External Evaluation Panel approval + SAB written non-objection**. Prevents post-hoc rationalization.

**Expanded from 8 to 11 axes per SAB feedback** — adds immune competence (K9), glucose dynamics under load (K10), sleep architecture (K11). These capture domains the program's own thesis depends on.

| KPI | Metric | Method | Baseline (M0-M2) | Success threshold (M16) | Stat test |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| **K1** | VO₂max (mL/kg/min) | Cosmed K5, ramp protocol on treadmill | Measured | +20% absolute OR top 0.1% age-matched | Paired pre-post; α=0.01 |
| **K2** | Peak watts on 6-s sprint | Wattbike Atom | Measured | +25% absolute OR top 0.1% age-matched | Paired pre-post |
| **K3** | TTE at 85% VO₂max | Treadmill, fixed protocol | Measured | +30% absolute | Paired pre-post |
| **K4** | FFM/Total mass ratio | DEXA + ADP | Measured | +5 percentage points absolute | Paired pre-post |
| **K5** | Cognitive composite Z (n-back, RAVEN, RT) | CANTAB + Cogstate | Measured | Top 0.1% age-matched on composite | Reference population |
| **K6** | DNAm GrimAge | TruDiagnostic | Measured | −3 years vs chronological at M16 | Single timepoint vs reference |
| **K7** | Inflammatory + metabolic composite (hsCRP, fasting insulin, HOMA-IR, IL-6, ApoB) | Standard labs | Measured | All 5 in top decile age-matched | Reference population |
| **K8** | HRV recovery slope post stress test | Clinical ECG | Measured | Top 5% age-matched | Reference population |
| **K9** [NEW] | **Immune competence composite**: vaccine challenge response (zoster + Tdap antibody titers + T-cell ELISpot), CMV-specific T-cell exhaustion markers, naïve:memory CD8 ratio, thymic output (sjTREC) | Vaccine challenge + immunophenotyping flow | Measured + baseline vaccine response | Composite within or above top decile age-matched; **NO decrement vs M0 baseline on any sub-component** | Multi-criterion AND test |
| **K10** [NEW] | **Glucose dynamics under load**: OGTT (Matsuda index) + 14-day CGM-derived variability (CV%) under structured exercise stress | OGTT + 14-day CGM with prescribed exercise | Measured | Matsuda ≥top decile age-matched AND CGM CV% ≤8% | AND test |
| **K11** [NEW] | **Sleep architecture composite**: SWS minutes, REM %, sleep spindle density, overnight HRV recovery | In-home PSG, 7 nights, baseline + M16 | Measured | SWS minutes +15% absolute OR top decile age-matched on composite | Composite Z-score |

**Aggregate success: ≥6 of 11 KPIs hit threshold.**

**Secondary/exploratory metrics (~40 more)** measured but not gating. They inform Phase 1 design.

## 8.7 Anti-Cherrypicking Safeguards (Strengthened)

1. **All KPIs pre-registered** publicly at M0 on OSF or equivalent
2. **Statistical Analysis Plan locked** by Head of Biostat before unblinding data
3. **External SAB review at M17 + EEC sign-off** before Board decision at G4
4. **EEP gate report delivered 30 days pre-gate** to Principal + LID + SAB Chair + Cook Islands trustee
5. **Independent third-party labs** for ≥50% of critical biomarkers (TruDiagnostic, Quest, etc.)
6. **Negative results published** internally; considered in spinout pivot decisions
7. **Audit trail** on all data — no editing of raw data, only of analyses, version controlled
8. **Pre-committed reasons for failure** documented; if resolved and KPIs still missed, thesis is wrong, not execution
9. **Public protocol pre-registration** is the most effective check on drift — F5 mitigation
10. **Pre-mortem at each gate** with external red-teamers; required appendix to gate decision memo
