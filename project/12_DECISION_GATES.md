# 12 — Decision Gates (v1.1, Post-SAB Review)

## Purpose

Decision Gates are pre-committed go/no-go decision points with documented criteria. They are the mechanism that prevents the program from drifting into a vanity project or escalating commitments without justified results.

**A gate is binding in the sense that:** ignoring it triggers Wind-Down Reserve forfeiture, EEC veto, Board resignation, public-pre-registration drift visibility, and program-wide reputational cost.

**A gate is NOT legally enforceable against the Principal.** Per Charter §1.4 honest acknowledgment, this is a sole-decider program with advisory + reputational + financial-friction counterweights.

**v1.1 revisions:** (a) Ulysses Contract added (§12.11); (b) $50M Wind-Down Reserve forfeiture mechanism; (c) External Evaluation Panel gate reports mandated; (d) Capacity sunset; (e) Age sunset; (f) AE sunset; (g) Public pre-registration as anti-drift mechanism; (h) Pre-mortem mandatory at each gate; (i) Whistleblower channel sanctioned alternative.

---

## 12.1 Gate Framework

Each gate has:
- **Trigger:** when evaluated
- **Decision Authority:** who decides
- **Pass Criteria:** numeric, falsifiable
- **Outcomes:** Pass / Conditional Pass / Fail
- **Escalation:** disagreement resolution

## 12.10 Emergency Gates (Unscheduled)

### EG1 — Emergency Pause (any party)
**Trigger:** Principal, any Board member, any SAB member, **any EEC member**, CEO, CMO, or GC requests pause
**Authority:** Single party can request; pause automatic and effective immediately
**Duration:** 30 days minimum
**Unpause:** Board majority + CMO + GC + EEC Chair concurrence

### EG2 — Adverse Event Review
**Trigger:** Grade 2+ AE in Principal (per recalibrated rules)
**Authority:** CMO unilateral pause authority; full review by Board + SAB + EEC + Ethics Council within 14 days
**Outcome:** Resume, modify protocol, or wind down

### EG3 — Regulatory Crisis Review
**Trigger:** Material regulatory action (criminal investigation, jurisdictional revocation, OFAC designation)
**Authority:** GC unilateral pause authority
**Outcome:** Relocate to co-primary jurisdiction, modify, or wind down

### EG4 — Reputational Crisis Review
**Trigger:** Major press disclosure
**Authority:** CEO + CSecO + GC convene crisis comms; Board called within 24 hours; **Posture Black activation** if needed per doc 17 BCP
**Outcome:** Communications strategy; possible voluntary pause

### EG5 — Principal Incapacitation
**Trigger:** Principal physically or mentally unable to participate; **OR capacity assessment falls >1.5 SD below baseline** (per §12.11.3 Capacity Sunset)
**Authority:** Designated medical proxy + Board + EEC
**Outcome:** Per advance directive (default: program pauses re: Principal interventions; institutional assets continue)

### EG6 — Whistleblower Disclosure (NEW)
**Trigger:** Whistleblower ombudsperson receives credible safety/integrity report
**Authority:** LID + EEP review within 7 days
**Outcome:** Investigation; potential pause; anti-retaliation enforcement

## 12.11 Ulysses Contract + Override Mechanism (NEW per SAB)

### 12.11.1 Ulysses Pre-Commitments

At Charter signing (G0), the Principal irrevocably pre-commits in writing:

1. **3-of-11 KPI outcome at G4** triggers automatic Phase-1 budget reduction to maintenance-only ($30M cap), reversible only after 6-month cooling-off + external review.
2. **2 Grade 3+ AEs within any rolling 24 months** triggers automatic 180-day pause and full Board+EEP review before any further escalation, with the burden of proof on continuation rather than wind-down.
3. **If cognitive composite declines >1.5 SD from personal baseline** (annually administered by external neuropsychologist contracted by EEP), the Principal's unilateral override authority on gate decisions is automatically suspended pending Board+EEP+proxy review. Principal retains bodily-autonomy authority but loses program-resource authority.
4. **At Principal age 75 (or 20 years from G0, whichever is earlier)**, the program enters mandatory Board-led reauthorization requiring affirmative re-consent + EEP review. Defaults to wind-down absent action.

### 12.11.2 Override Mechanism (Real Friction)

To override a Board+SAB+EEC "Fail" determination at G4–G7:

1. **Mandatory 90-day cooling-off**
2. **Written acknowledgment** of each specific SAB, Board, and EEC objection, item by item
3. **Commission + personally receive** second-opinion review from the standing External Evaluation Panel
4. **Automatic forfeiture of the $50M Wind-Down Reserve** to the institutional entity (not Principal's estate)
5. **Replenishment of Wind-Down Reserve to $50M** before any subsequent gate override (additional Principal capital commitment)

### 12.11.3 Documentation Retention (NEW)

All raw clinical data, decision memos, EEP reports, gate determinations, recusal logs preserved for **minimum 50 years** in three jurisdictionally separated copies (Cayman + Cook Islands + US/EU academic deposit such as escrowed institutional repository).

Access in failure scenarios:
- Named medical proxy gets clinical records
- LID + trustee jointly authorize broader release
- Regulatory or law-enforcement subpoena: defined GC-managed response protocol

This protects Principal's estate, future researchers, harmed parties.

## 12.12 Anti-Drift Mechanisms (Strengthened per SAB)

1. **Pre-registration is binding.** Criteria locked at M0 cannot be modified without EEP approval + SAB non-objection. Public pre-registration on OSF or equivalent.
2. **External SAB and EEC have no equity.** No commercial incentive to soften criteria.
3. **EEP paid from irrevocable escrow.** Cannot be defunded by unhappy Principal mid-program.
4. **Annual external audit** of all gate decisions; auditor reports to Principal + Board + EEC.
5. **Public pre-registration** of KPI thresholds on registered protocol database. Creates external accountability. F5 drift mitigation.
6. **Sunset clauses (§12.11.1):** AE sunset, capacity sunset, age sunset all bind automatically.
7. **Personal sunsets:** Each C-suite contract has clause that, if no phase transition for 36+ months, contract converts to maintenance-mode at 50% comp.
8. **Whistleblower channel** (outside ombudsperson, 24/7) is sanctioned alternative to leak. Quarterly reports to LID + EEP.
