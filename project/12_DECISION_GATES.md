# 12 — Decision Gates

## Purpose

Decision Gates are pre-committed go/no-go decision points with documented criteria. They are the mechanism that prevents the program from drifting into a vanity project or escalating commitments without justified results.

**A gate is binding.** If criteria are not met, the program does not proceed to the next phase. Criteria cannot be modified retroactively.

---

## 12.1 Gate Framework

Each gate has:
- **Trigger:** When the gate is evaluated
- **Decision Authority:** Who decides (always includes the Principal)
- **Pass Criteria:** Numeric, falsifiable conditions that must be met
- **Outcomes:** What happens for Pass / Conditional Pass / Fail
- **Escalation:** What happens if there is disagreement

## 12.10 Emergency Gates

The above are scheduled. There are also **unscheduled gates** that can be triggered by events.

### EG1 — Emergency Pause (any party)

**Trigger:** Principal, any Board member, any SAB member, CEO, CMO, or GC requests pause
**Authority:** Single party can request; pause is automatic and effective immediately
**Duration:** 30 days minimum
**Unpause:** Board majority + CMO + GC concurrence

### EG2 — Adverse Event Review

**Trigger:** Grade 3+ adverse event in Principal
**Authority:** CMO has unilateral pause authority; full review by Board + SAB + Ethics Council within 14 days
**Outcome:** Resume, modify protocol, or wind down

### EG3 — Regulatory Crisis Review

**Trigger:** Material regulatory action (criminal investigation, jurisdictional revocation, etc.)
**Authority:** GC has unilateral pause authority
**Outcome:** Relocate, modify, or wind down

### EG4 — Reputational Crisis Review

**Trigger:** Major press disclosure
**Authority:** CEO + CSecO + GC convene crisis comms; Board called within 24 hours
**Outcome:** Communications strategy; possible voluntary pause

### EG5 — Principal Incapacitation Review

**Trigger:** Principal physically or mentally unable to participate
**Authority:** Designated medical proxy + Board
**Outcome:** Per advance directive (default: program pauses with respect to Principal interventions; institutional assets continue)

## 12.11 Anti-Drift Mechanisms

The single largest threat to program integrity is **drift**: incremental criterion-relaxation that eventually accumulates into a vanity project.

Counter-measures:

1. **Pre-registration is binding.** Criteria locked at Month 0 cannot be modified without external SAB approval.
2. **External SAB members have no equity** in spinouts. They have no commercial incentive to soften criteria.
3. **Annual external audit** of all gate decisions; auditor reports to Principal and Board.
4. **Public pre-registration** of the KPI thresholds (within commercial confidentiality limits) on a registered protocol database. This creates external accountability.
5. **Sunset clause:** If no phase transition for >36 months, program automatically enters wind-down review.
6. **Personal sunsets:** Each C-suite member's contract has a clause that, if no phase transition for 36+ months, contract converts to maintenance-mode terms at 50% comp.

## 12.12 The Honest Failure Pathway

If Gate G4 fails at Month 18 (less than 6 of 8 KPIs), the most likely scenario, here is the wind-down:

**Months 18–24:**
- Spinouts retained; capital concentrates on commercialization
- Half of scientific staff retained for ongoing spinout R&D
- Próspera operations scaled to spinout-supporting only
- Audit complete; lessons documented
- Principal's protocol continues as longevity maintenance (non-aggressive)

**Months 24–36:**
- Spinout exits begin
- Holdco transitions to pure investment / royalty vehicle
- Staff continues with spinouts (carried over)
- Principal maintains lifestyle + non-experimental protocol

**Terminal state:**
- Principal has documented elite-level optimization (won't be 100× human, but possibly 2× elite)
- Holdco has $100M–$1B in spinout equity / proceeds
- Total cost: $42–60M
- IP retained
- Ethics record clean
- Principal continues being a wealthy, optimized individual rather than a posthuman one

**This is the "lose gracefully" outcome. It is documented, planned, and economically rational.**

## 12.14 Final Principle

**Every gate is real.** No criterion is decorative. No outcome is rationalized post-hoc.

This is the discipline that separates Project PROMETHEUS from every prior posthuman vanity project. We do not iterate indefinitely on a failed thesis. We do not move goalposts. We do not let the founder's pride override the data.

**The Principal's authority is absolute over their own body. The gates' authority is absolute over the program's resources.**

These two principles together produce a program that can credibly run for 25 years without drifting into either a billionaire's vanity or a runaway research apparatus. They are non-negotiable.
