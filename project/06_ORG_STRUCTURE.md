### 6.3.2 Chief Medical Officer вҖ” REVISED FOR INDEPENDENCE

**Profile:** MD-PhD with longevity/aging clinical experience AND research credentials. Active medical license in **at least two jurisdictions** (PrГіspera + ideally US, UK, or UAE). 10+ years patient care, 5+ years clinical research leadership.

**Independence Structure (NEW per SAB):**
- **3-year non-terminable contract** except for cause determined by EEC (not Principal, not Board)
- **Compensation fully prepaid into escrow at contract start**, paid out quarterly regardless of program status
- **Tail payment** if CMO halts the program for safety reasons (positive incentive to call halt)
- CMO carries **personal professional indemnity insurance funded by program but owned by CMO**
- CMO has **contractually enumerated whistleblower protections** including guaranteed severance + NDA carve-out
- License held in functioning medical board jurisdiction (not solely PrГіspera) so external professional accountability exists
- CMO cannot hold equity in spinouts whose products they approve for Principal use (categorical exclusion)
- CMO base + bonus prepaid; equity is **phantom Holdco units only** (not specific spinouts)

**Veto authority** absolute on procedures on Principal. **Cannot be unilaterally fired by Principal during 3-year term.**

**Backup plan:** **Shadow CMO retained from Day 7** ($50K/yr stipend) вҖ” pre-identified concierge medicine senior physician, can step in within 2 weeks. Two associate CMOs by Month 12 for 24/7 coverage.

## 6.5 Cultural Operating Principles (Expanded)

1. **The Principal's safety is the only constraint we never violate.** Everything else is negotiable.
2. **Falsifiability beats optimism.** Every claim has a numeric failure condition.
3. **No silos.** Cross-team communication is mandatory; secrecy is *external*, not internal вҖ” within need-to-know rules.
4. **Speed of execution > perfection of plan.** Decisions at the lowest competent level.
5. **Document everything.** If it isn't written down, it didn't happen.
6. **No politics.** Personal disputes go to manager + HR same-day. Veto-deadlock to S&S Cmte same-week.
7. **Pre-commit to gates.** If a gate is missed, we pivot or stop. We do not retroactively redefine success.
8. **Spinouts win even when integration doesn't.** Every component earns its keep independently.
9. **The work is real even if the goal sounds absurd.** DEFINITIONS.md is the operational target. Anyone who can't take it seriously doesn't belong.
10. **Don't lie to the Principal.** Especially when data is bad. This is contractually enforceable per CMO escrow.
11. **(NEW) Honest internal disagreement is required.** "Capture by Principal" or "groupthink toward Principal preference" is the failure mode. Whistleblower ombudsperson is the sanctioned channel.

## 6.7 Diversity & Counterintelligence (Expanded)

Deliberately diversify hires across nationalities. **No single nationality >30%** prevents simple state-actor infiltration.

**Insider Threat Working Group (NEW):**
- UEBA on all program-network endpoints (Exabeam/Vectra-tier)
- Continuous evaluation (not one-shot pre-hire) for Tier-Red access (~15 staff)
- Foreign-contact reporting requirements
- Financial-lifestyle monitoring (consented, condition of access)
- Travel reporting for senior + Tier-Red staff
- 90-day post-departure monitoring window
- Quarterly debriefs with CSecO + CI Officer for senior staff

Budget: ~$1.2M/yr (covered in doc 04 security line).

## 6.8 Succession Planning (Strengthened)

**Phase 0 Month 0** (not Month 12 as v1.0): Every C-suite role has a **named contracted shadow on retainer** ($50K/yr each). Total: ~$300K/yr for full shadow C-suite from Day 1.

By **Month 6**: Every C-suite has a Day-1-capable deputy (full-time). Continuity binders quarterly. Tested handoff procedure.

By **Month 24**: Every senior PI has a deputy capable of 90 days unsupervised. All protocols at SOP-level documentation.

**Principal succession (advance directive):**
- **Primary medical proxy:** Named individual, **not** CMO, **not** program-employed, **not** Board member. Long-standing personal physician or senior physician on retainer specifically as proxy. Paid from trust escrow.
- **Alternate proxy:** Second named individual, geographically separated.
- **Tertiary proxy:** LID + Cook Islands trustee acting jointly, only if both named proxies unreachable within 72 hours.
- **Annual proxy review:** in-person rehearsal with CMO + GC + named proxies.

In event of Principal incapacitation: Cook Islands trustee + Board jointly authorize continuation of institutional assets (spinouts, IP, talent) even if Principal's personal participation ends. Advance directive specifies whether and how program continues.
