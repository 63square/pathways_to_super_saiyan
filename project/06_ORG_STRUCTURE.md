## 6.3 Role Specifications

### 6.3.1 Chief Executive / Program Director

**Profile:** MD-PhD or PhD with operational leadership experience at biotech ≥$1B valuation or research institute ≥500 staff. 15+ years operational, 5+ years P&L. Pattern of building multidisciplinary teams.

**Candidates to consider:**
- Former CEO of a Series C+ longevity company (Altos COO-level, Retro CEO-level, etc.)
- Former director of a major translational research institute (Wyss, Broad equivalent)
- Former senior leader at a defense biotech (DARPA BTO, IARPA)

**KPIs:** Phase gate completion, budget adherence, talent retention, Principal satisfaction.

### 6.3.2 Chief Medical Officer

**Profile:** MD with longevity/aging clinical experience AND research credentials. Active medical license in at least one jurisdiction (Próspera + ideally US or UK). 10+ years patient care, 5+ years clinical research leadership.

**Specific must-haves:**
- Experience running interventional trials in healthy adults
- Familiarity with off-label use frameworks
- Track record on AAV gene therapy clinical safety OR cell therapy clinical safety
- Comfortable with concierge / executive medicine model

**Veto authority** on any procedure on the Principal. Reports administratively to CEO but has direct line to Principal.

**Backup plan:** Two associate CMOs by Month 12 to ensure 24/7/365 medical coverage; succession plan if CMO incapacitated.

### 6.3.3 Chief Scientific Officer

**Profile:** PhD with substantial multidisciplinary leadership. Former chair of department or director of major institute. H-index ≥50 preferred. Track record on translating basic science to clinical.

**Domains of fluency required:**
- Regenerative medicine / reprogramming
- Gene therapy
- Aging biology
- Performance physiology
- Computational biology / AI

**Reports to CEO.** Manages 3–4 PIs in Phase 0, scaling to 15+ by Phase 2.

### 6.3.4 General Counsel

**Profile:** JD with biotech regulatory + international structuring expertise. Big-firm partner background or equivalent (Wilson Sonsini, Cooley, Goodwin). 10+ years on bio matters.

**Must have:** familiarity with FDA + EMA + offshore (Cayman, BVI, Próspera) corporate structures. International trust law experience.

**Veto authority** on any action with regulatory exposure or legal risk to Principal.

### 6.3.5 Chief Financial Officer

**Profile:** CPA or equivalent with $1B+ biotech CFO experience. Captive insurance experience. Cap table management for venture-backed companies. International tax sophistication.

### 6.3.6 Chief Security Officer

**Profile:** Former intelligence community (CIA DST, MI6 H, Mossad equivalents) OR former Tier 1 special operations with corporate security experience. Cyber expertise paired with physical security. Has run executive protection for >$1B principal previously.

### 6.3.7 PMO Director

**Profile:** PMP/PgMP certified. Run programs ≥$100M. Biotech, defense, or major construction background. Reports to CEO.

### 6.3.8 Principal Investigators (Phase 0: 3)

**PI-Reprogramming:** Faculty-level. From Altos, Retro, NewLimit, Salk, Buck, or Babraham. Has published in *Cell*/*Nature*/*Science* on epigenetic age reversal.

**PI-Bioenergetics:** Faculty-level. From Wallace lab, McCully lab, Spiegelman lab, or equivalent. Track record on mitochondrial transplantation or PGC-1α biology.

**PI-Performance Physiology:** Olympic/professional-sport medicine experience. MD or PhD. Has overseen elite athlete enhancement programs (legally, ideally).

(PI-Gene Therapy added Phase 1.)

### Hiring Compensation Philosophy

- **Cash:** Competitive with top 25% of biotech market
- **Equity:** Above-market for senior roles (founder vesting on C-suite); spinout equity pool funded at 15% diluted
- **Benefits:** Premium; concierge medical for all staff (program-relevant); life insurance 5× salary; family healthcare; relocation; retention bonuses every 24 months
- **Working environment:** Próspera as a draw (tax advantages for international hires), or remote-first for non-wet-lab roles, or Boston/Bay Area satellite for talent that won't relocate
- **Prestige:** Publications encouraged where program allows; SAB seat for retired distinguished staff; Nobel-track work is genuinely available here

### Non-Negotiable Hiring Standards

- Background check (criminal, credit, social media, reference) on every hire including C-suite
- Polygraph (for CSecO + key program leaders only; voluntary but expected)
- Drug screen pre-employment + random
- NDA on Day 1 with criminal clause
- Non-compete (where enforceable) + non-solicit globally for 2 years
- No "rest and vest" tolerated; quarterly contribution reviews

## 6.5 Cultural Operating Principles

These are the rules everyone in the program operates by. Posted in every office.

1. **The Principal's safety is the only constraint we never violate.** Everything else is negotiable.
2. **Falsifiability beats optimism.** Every claim has a numeric failure condition.
3. **No silos.** Cross-team communication is mandatory; secrecy is *external*, not internal.
4. **Speed of execution > perfection of plan.** Decisions are made at the lowest competent level.
5. **Document everything.** If it isn't written down, it didn't happen.
6. **No politics.** Personal disputes go to manager + HR same-day.
7. **Pre-commit to gates.** If a gate is missed, we pivot or stop. We do not retroactively redefine success.
8. **Spinouts win even when the integration doesn't.** Every component earns its keep independently.
9. **The work is real even if the goal sounds absurd.** Anyone who can't take both seriously doesn't belong here.
10. **Don't lie to the Principal.** Especially when the data is bad.

## 6.6 Performance Management

- **Quarterly OKRs** at company, function, and individual level
- **360 reviews** at C-suite annually, function leads bi-annually, individuals annually
- **Calibration** across functions to prevent grade inflation
- **Termination is calibrated and respectful** — but happens. We are not a tenured environment.
- **Underperformers exit within 90 days** of formal warning

## 6.7 Diversity & Counterintelligence

We deliberately diversify hires across nationalities for two reasons:
- Talent excellence is globally distributed
- **No single nationality >30%** prevents simple state-actor infiltration

Specific anti-counterintelligence measures:
- Background check vendors with intelligence community linkage
- Compartmentalization: only ~5 people see the full integration thesis
- Air-gapped strategic IP; need-to-know access
- Quarterly debriefs with CSecO for senior staff
- Travel reporting (to high-risk countries) for senior staff

## 6.8 Succession Planning

By Month 12 of Phase 0, every C-suite role must have:
- Documented deputy (#2) with full operational competence
- Continuity binder updated quarterly
- Tested handoff procedure

By Month 24, every senior PI must have:
- A deputy capable of running the lab for 90 days unsupervised
- All protocols documented at SOP-level

The Principal's role itself has a succession plan: in event of incapacitation, the Cook Islands trustee + Board jointly authorize continuation of the institutional asset (spinouts, IP, talent) even if the Principal's personal participation ends. See doc 07 for legal mechanics.
