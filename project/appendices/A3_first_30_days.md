# Appendix A3 — First 30 Days Operational Plan

## Purpose

The first 30 days after Charter signing are the most leveraged period of the program. Documentation, structures, and key relationships founded in Days 1–30 determine the program's character for years.

This appendix is the day-by-day playbook.

---

## Pre-Day-1 Prerequisites (Principal preparation)

Before signing the Charter, the Principal must complete:

- [ ] Read all 13 numbered project documents
- [ ] Engage personal independent legal counsel (not GC) for review
- [ ] Engage personal independent medical counsel for review
- [ ] Confirm capital availability ($7M liquid for T1, $52M committed)
- [ ] Identify Board candidates (5 seats; Principal proposes, Board candidates accept)
- [ ] Identify candidate trustees for Cook Islands trust
- [ ] Confirm willingness to spend ~10 hrs/month time commitment in Phase 0 Months 1–3
- [ ] Designate medical proxy for advance directive

---

## Week 1 (Days 1–7)

### Day 1 — Charter Signing
- [ ] Principal signs Project Charter (doc 01)
- [ ] Principal signs initial Master Consent (template in doc 11)
- [ ] T1 wire transferred ($7M) to interim escrow
- [ ] PMO Director (interim, contracted from PM firm) onboarded as Day-1 ops lead
- [ ] Executive search firms engaged (Spencer Stuart for CEO; Korn Ferry Healthcare for CMO)
- [ ] IP firm engaged (Wilson Sonsini Bio practice on retainer)
- [ ] Tax counsel engaged

### Days 2–4 — Entity Initiation
- [ ] Cayman Holdco incorporation initiated (4–6 week process); registered agent retained
- [ ] Cook Islands trust drafted (Principal's personal counsel reviews)
- [ ] Próspera consultation scheduled (regulatory pre-engagement)
- [ ] Bermuda captive insurance preliminary discussion
- [ ] Delaware LLC for US operations drafted

### Days 5–7 — First Hires
- [ ] PMO Director offer accepted (full-time, Day-7 start at latest)
- [ ] Executive search briefing meetings (CEO, CMO, CSO, GC, CFO searches launched)
- [ ] Initial SAB outreach (Principal + PMO sends invitations to 12 candidates targeting 9 final seats)
- [ ] First weekly Principal–PMO standup (Friday)

## Risks in First 30 Days

| Risk | Probability | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|
| Cayman Holdco incorporation slips past Day 30 | 30% | Use ready-made Cayman shell + amend; or expedited processing |
| CEO offer rejected by top candidate | 25% | Backup candidate pre-identified; signing bonus authorized |
| Próspera regulatory environment changes mid-process | 10% | Cayman backup engagement begun in parallel Day 15 |
| Principal availability for Days 22–25 site visit conflicts | 20% | Pre-schedule on Charter signing; visit can shift ±1 week |
| First SAB candidates decline (academic schedules) | 35% | Over-invite (12 for 9 seats); fallback list of 6 more |

---

## Reporting Cadence (Days 1–30)

- **Daily:** PMO posts status to shared dashboard
- **Weekly (Friday):** Principal + PMO 1-hour standup; written summary
- **Biweekly:** Personal counsel and medical counsel briefed
- **Day 14:** Mid-month status to Principal
- **Day 30:** Comprehensive 30-day report to Principal; spending reconciliation; Day 30–60 plan
