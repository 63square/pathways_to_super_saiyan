# 02 — Feasibility Analysis

## Purpose

To partition every intervention proposed in Volumes I and II of the white paper into four buckets:

- **GREEN — In Pilot (Phase 0):** Published in humans, safe profile, deliverable now
- **YELLOW — In Foundation (Phase 1–2):** Demonstrated in primates/humans for related indications, requires protocol development
- **ORANGE — In Extension (Phase 3+):** Plausible but requires substantial engineering and regulatory work, deferred
- **RED — Cut from program:** Speculative, unfalsifiable, or violates physical law; remains research curiosity only

This is the central document. Every other document derives from this triage.

## 2.2 Why the Cuts Matter

The original two-volume white paper conflated three categories that a serious program must keep separate:

1. **Things we can do now, that have biomarker-grade evidence in humans.** (GREEN)
2. **Things we can develop within a 5–10 year program.** (YELLOW + ORANGE)
3. **Things that may turn out to be possible if frontier physics or biology cooperates.** (RED)

Treating all three as deliverables is the failure mode of every prior posthuman program (cf. Howard Hughes' later work, Theranos). **Project PROMETHEUS operates only on categories 1 and 2 with capital. Category 3 is monitored as research, not pursued as engineering.**

## 2.3 Multiplicative Realism Check

Volume II claimed 50–1000× over baseline elite human. Let's pressure-test with only GREEN + YELLOW interventions:

| Intervention | Realistic multiplier (lower bound) | Source |
|---|---|---|
| Genetic baseline already elite (Principal is presumed top-decile) | 1.0× (baseline) | — |
| AAV myostatin/ActRII (Y1, Y2) | 1.3–1.7× lean mass | Phase 2 data |
| Partial reprogramming (Y3) | 1.5–2× recovery capacity | Mouse data, conservative |
| Mitochondrial transplant (Y4) | 1.3–2× VO₂max ceiling | Pre-clinical |
| Endocrine optimization (G9) | 1.3–1.6× FFM | Bhasin 1996 + modern protocols |
| Senolytics + TPE (G2+G3) | 1.2–1.5× recovery; longevity | Hickson 2019 |
| Klotho (Y5) | 1.2–1.4× cognitive resilience | Dubal 2014 |
| BCI skill acceleration (Y10) | 2–3× effective practice efficiency | Speculative but defensible |
| HBOT + structured training (G7+G8) | 1.2–1.5× endurance | Hachmo 2020 |
| Lifespan extension (Y3+Y6+Y8) | 1.5–2× career duration | Field consensus |
| Microbiome (G5) | 1.05–1.15× endurance | Scheiman 2019 |

**Conservative product (taking lower bounds):** 1.3 × 1.5 × 1.3 × 1.3 × 1.2 × 1.2 × 2.0 × 1.2 × 1.5 × 1.05 ≈ **12×** over the Principal's baseline.
**Optimistic product (taking upper bounds):** ≈ **80×** over baseline.

**12×–80× is not Super Saiyan.** It is, however, comfortably superhuman by any 2026 metric. It is also exactly what the multiplicative argument promises when restricted to interventions we can defend in front of an SAB.

Phase 3+ adds materials and cybernetics, which can plausibly push this another 5–10× **on specific metrics** (impact tolerance, reaction time, projected energy). Total terminal multiplier: 60–800×, lopsided across metrics.

**This is the realistic envelope. It is not the canonical Super Saiyan envelope. It is dramatically better than any existing human.**

## 2.4 What "Feasibility" Means Here

For every GREEN and YELLOW item we require:
1. ≥1 peer-reviewed publication demonstrating the intervention in humans or in a mammalian model considered translational (NHP, dog, pig)
2. ≥1 currently-active commercial or academic program working on the same intervention (de-risks the science by social proof)
3. A clear path to GMP-equivalent manufacturing or sourcing
4. A clear path to regulatory authorization (either via the Próspera Ethics Council framework, FDA, EMA, or compassionate use)
5. A documented safety profile with quantified adverse event rates from prior trials

For ORANGE items we require:
1. Plausible engineering path with no physical-law violations
2. Identifiable critical-path technology that is already on a development trajectory
3. Demonstrable scaling pathway from current state to required state

For RED items we monitor academic literature quarterly. No capital allocated.

## 2.5 Falsifiability

Every intervention in GREEN and YELLOW has at least one quantitative biomarker that can fail. The Phase 0 KPIs (doc 08) are pre-registered with specific numeric thresholds. If those thresholds are not met, the corresponding intervention is **defunded** at the Phase 0–1 gate, not iterated indefinitely.

This is the discipline that distinguishes Project PROMETHEUS from a vanity project. **Every assertion has a failure condition.**
