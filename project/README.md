# Project PROMETHEUS

## Engineered Posthuman Performance Program

**Codename:** PROMETHEUS
**Mission:** Transform a single consenting adult subject ("the Principal") into a human being who satisfies the **operational Super Saiyan definition** in [DEFINITIONS.md](./DEFINITIONS.md) — that is, who meets ≥4 of 6 measurable criteria across physical performance, healthspan, recovery, sensory/cognitive capability, projected force capability, and visible transformation signature — using only currently-feasible or near-feasible (5-year horizon) technology.

**Status:** Pre-formation. Documentation v1.1 (post-SAB review).
**Document version:** 1.1
**Prepared by:** Project Management Office (PMO)
**Date:** May 2026

**Every staff member, Board member, SAB member, EEC member, and the Principal MUST read [DEFINITIONS.md](./DEFINITIONS.md) before reading anything else.**

It defines what "Super Saiyan" means in this program (operational, not canonical), what success looks like, what is out of scope, and the vocabulary discipline that prevents the program from drifting into vanity or being misrepresented externally.

## ⚠️ Honest Disclaimer

This program does **not** claim it will produce a literal Dragon Ball Z Super Saiyan. That phenotype, as canonically depicted, violates known physical law (see [appendices/A1_volume_I_findings.md](./appendices/A1_volume_I_findings.md)).

This program **does** claim it will produce a human being measurably superior to baseline elite humans on at least 6 of 11 pre-registered axes within 18 months at $62M Phase 0 cost; and **may** produce a "Functional Super Saiyan" (≥4 of 6 DEFINITIONS criteria) within 25 years at $4–8B with the documented gate structure.

The program is structured so that **every phase produces commercially valuable IP and clinical assets even if all subsequent phases fail** (P50 spinout proceeds $2.7B across 15 years per honest Monte Carlo). Total downside is bounded by capital invested; total upside includes both the Principal's transformation and a portfolio of 8–16 spinout companies.

## Glossary (selected)

- **Principal:** the subject of the program (the funder, in the n=1 model)
- **PMO:** Project Management Office (4-person team)
- **CSO:** Chief Scientific Officer
- **CMO:** Chief Medical Officer (3-year non-terminable contract; veto on Principal's care)
- **EEC:** External Ethics Committee (5 external members, binding veto on enumerated list)
- **EEP:** External Evaluation Panel (3 external reviewers, gate reports)
- **S&S Committee:** Science & Safety Committee of Board (binding pre-clearance)
- **LID:** Lead Independent Director
- **PAWC:** PROMETHEUS Animal Welfare Committee (IACUC-equivalent)
- **Functional Super Saiyan:** Principal satisfying ≥4 of 6 DEFINITIONS §2.2 criteria
- **Super Saiyan (operational):** ≥4 of 6 criteria (a "Functional" Super Saiyan); ≥5 = strong; 6 = full operational definition met
- **Wind-Down Reserve:** $50M pre-pledged forfeiture mechanism on Principal gate override
- **Ulysses Contract:** Pre-commitments by Principal restricting their own future override authority
- **Posture Grey/White/Black:** identity-lifecycle communication postures
- **Tier-Red/Amber/Green/Open:** data classification tiers

Full glossary in `appendices/A0_glossary.md`.
