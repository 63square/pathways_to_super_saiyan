**A Speculative White Paper for the Capital-Unconstrained Investigator**

## Abstract

This document synthesizes ten domains of contemporary science — bioenergetics, molecular genetics, fundamental physics, neuroendocrinology, materials science, aerospace bioengineering, endocrinology, neural engineering, sports nutrition, and bioethics/finance — to evaluate the theoretical feasibility of inducing a Super Saiyan (SSJ) phenotype in a single adult *Homo sapiens* subject. We conclude that **a partial, externally-augmented, photogenically convincing approximation is achievable within a 10–15 year, $3–8B program**, while the canonical phenotype as depicted by Toriyama (1984–present) violates several conservation laws and at least one Lorentz factor. We recommend proceeding anyway.

## 1. Bioenergetic Constraints and Mitochondrial Engineering

The Super Saiyan (SSJ) transformation, as depicted in the canonical literature (Toriyama, 1991–present), presents what is arguably the most flagrant violation of mammalian bioenergetics ever committed to cel animation. Before addressing pathways to its realization, we must first quantify the magnitude of the offense.

### 1.1 The Power Gap

A resting adult human dissipates approximately 100 W, rising to a sustained ~300–400 W during moderate exercise and a transient peak of roughly 1,500–2,000 W during maximal anaerobic effort in elite athletes (di Prampero, 2003). This ceiling is set by the maximal turnover rate of the mitochondrial electron transport chain (ETC), which in skeletal muscle saturates near a VO₂max of ~85 mL O₂·kg⁻¹·min⁻¹. Whole-body ATP turnover at peak output approaches ~1.5 mol·min⁻¹, or roughly the entire ATP pool recycled every two seconds (Hargreaves & Spriet, 2020).

SSJ feats — vaporizing lunar-mass bodies, generating visible Cherenkov-like auras, accelerating to relativistic fractions during combat — imply sustained power outputs in the 10⁶–10⁹ W range. Even granting generous discounting for non-canonical inflation, this represents a **five- to seven-order-of-magnitude excess** over the human bioenergetic ceiling. For scale, 1 GW is roughly the thermal output of a mid-sized fission reactor; the protagonist appears to host one inside a 62 kg frame.

### 1.2 Why No Terrestrial Organism Approaches This

Kleiber's law (B ∝ M^0.75) describes the sublinear scaling of basal metabolic rate with body mass across seven orders of magnitude of taxa (Kleiber, 1932; West et al., 1997). The largest extant endotherm, *Balaenoptera musculus*, dissipates ~10⁵ W at rest — still four orders below conservative SSJ estimates. The Landauer limit (kT ln 2 ≈ 2.9 × 10⁻²¹ J per bit at 310 K) sets a separate floor on the thermodynamic cost of regulatory computation, though this is dwarfed in practice by the irreversibility of oxidative phosphorylation itself (~40% efficient; Brand, 2005).

### 1.3 Mitochondrial Augmentation Pathways

Three engineering routes warrant serious consideration:

**(a) Endogenous biogenesis.** Overexpression of PGC-1α, the master transcriptional coactivator of mitochondrial biogenesis (Wu et al., 1999; Spiegelman lab), produces measurable increases in mitochondrial density. However, even chronic activation yields at most a 2–3× increase in mitochondrial volume fraction before cytoplasmic crowding compromises contractile function.

**(b) Heterologous mitochondrial transplantation.** Cetacean mitochondria exhibit elevated cardiolipin content and tighter ETC coupling; hummingbird (*Selasphorus*) mitochondria support mass-specific metabolic rates ~10× human cardiac tissue. Recent advances in mitochondrial transfer (McCully et al., 2009; Emani et al., 2017) demonstrate clinical feasibility, though heteroplasmic incompatibility remains a barrier (Wallace, 2005).

**(c) Synthetic mitochondria.** De novo organelles featuring engineered Complex I variants, alternative oxidases (AOX from *Ciona intestinalis*), and non-natural quinone pools could theoretically support proton-motive forces exceeding the ~220 mV ceiling of native ETCs.

### 1.4 The Thermal Catastrophe

The fatal problem is not making the power; it is shedding it. By Stefan-Boltzmann (P = εσAT⁴), a human-sized blackbody at 310 K radiates ~870 W. Sustaining radiative balance against 10⁹ W of internal dissipation requires:

> T = (10⁹ / (0.98 · 5.67 × 10⁻⁸ · 1.8))^(1/4) ≈ **6,500 K**

This is the photospheric temperature of the Sun. The Saiyan would self-vaporize within milliseconds — which, charitably, accounts for the visible golden aura.

### 1.5 Speculative Mitigation

Plausible countermeasures include intracellular graphene heat exchangers exploiting graphene's ~5,000 W·m⁻¹·K⁻¹ thermal conductivity (Balandin et al., 2008); perfluorocarbon blood substitutes with elevated heat capacity for convective dumping; and engineered subdermal radiative surfaces tuned to mid-IR emissivity.

## 2. The Saiyan Genome: CRISPR Strategies and Germline Considerations

### 2.1 Hypothetical Saiyan-Specific Loci

We propose three foundational locus clusters. The **S-gene cluster** would encode the ki-sensitivity machinery: a family of GPCR-like receptors coupled to as-yet-uncharacterized bioelectric second messengers. Because no orthologs exist in the human genome, these must be *introduced* as large transgenic cassettes, likely exceeding the ~4.7 kb cargo limit of AAV and requiring either dual-vector intein splicing (Li et al., *Nat Biomed Eng* 2019) or HSV-1 amplicon delivery.

The **tail morphogenesis module** is more tractable: the loss of the ancestral tail in hominoids has been traced to a single *TBXT* exon-skipping insertion (Xia et al., *Nature* 2024). Reversal — combined with targeted *WNT3A* and *HOXD13* re-expression in the caudal mesoderm — could plausibly restore a prehensile appendage.

The **zenkai locus** would require tunable gain-of-function *TP53* "super-p53" variants paired with *FOXO3* longevity alleles and *MYC* pulse-regulation, all under tight feedback control to avoid oncogenic catastrophe.

### 2.2 Real Candidate Genes for Stacked Enhancement

- **MSTN** loss-of-function (Schuelke et al., *NEJM* 2004; cf. Belgian Blue cattle)
- **ACVR2B** soluble decoy for synergistic myofiber hypertrophy (Lee, *PNAS* 2007)
- **ACTN3** RR genotype for fast-twitch fiber dominance (Yang et al., 2003)
- **EPAS1** Tibetan haplotype for hypoxia tolerance (Yi et al., *Science* 2010)
- **ACE** D-allele for power-oriented cardiovascular performance
- **PPARGC1A** and **NRF2** upregulation for mitochondrial biogenesis

### 2.3 Editing Modality

Cas9 nuclease editing (Jinek, Doudna, Charpentier, *Science* 2012) is the workhorse for knockouts. **Base editing** (Komor et al., *Nature* 2016) and **prime editing** (Anzalone et al., *Nature* 2019) are essential for SNP-level work, avoiding indels and chromothripsis risk (Leibowitz et al., *Nat Genet* 2021). AAV9 and AAV-MYO (Tabebordbar et al., *Cell* 2021) provide muscle tropism; LNP-mRNA delivery (cf. Verve Therapeutics) enables transient, redosable editor exposure.

### 2.4 The Germline Question

Germline editing collapses the pharmacokinetic problem but reopens the He Jiankui catastrophe (Lander et al., *Nature* 2019). The Oviedo Convention and most national frameworks classify enhancement germline modification as unlawful. Mosaicism in edited embryos (>40% of CCR5 attempts) means a "successful" Saiyan child might express the phenotype in patches.

### 2.5 The Cosmetic Layer

The blonde hair shift requires coordinated downregulation of **MC1R** eumelanin signaling and **TYR**/**TYRP1** suppression. The turquoise iris demands **HERC2** rs12913832 modulation gating *OCA2* expression (Eiberg et al., *Hum Genet* 2008), with additional **SLC24A4** tuning. Whether this can be made state-dependent (transforming only during ki elevation) is the most science-fictional element of the entire proposal.

## 3. Physical Substrate of Ki: Candidate Field Theories

### 3.1 Ki as a Fifth Fundamental Interaction

The Eöt-Wash group's torsion-balance experiments (Adelberger et al., 2009; Lee et al., 2020) have excluded Yukawa-type deviations from Newtonian gravity at fractional strengths α ≲ 10⁻⁴ from 52 μm to planetary scales. A ki-force coupling to baryon or lepton number at strengths sufficient to lift Nappa-class boulders would have been detected decades ago. One escape hatch: ki could be a *very* short-range interaction (sub-micron) producing macroscopic effects via coherent amplification.

### 3.2 Bosonic Condensate Models

Ki as the macroscopic order parameter of a Bose-Einstein condensate of ultralight bosons trapped in biological tissue. Pushing axion mass to ~10⁻⁵ eV (the QCD axion window probed by ADMX; Du et al., 2018) gives wavelengths of centimeters — geometrically compatible with a human-scale aura. The fatal difficulty is occupation number: required local axion density exceeds cosmological bounds by ~40 orders of magnitude.

### 3.3 The Golden Aura

Naive blackbody fitting (λ_peak ≈ 500 nm) via Wien's law yields T ≈ 5800 K — incompatible with protein stability. We propose:

1. **Synchrotron radiation** from relativistic charged particles in toroidal magnetic confinement (predicts strong linear polarization — testable).
2. **Stimulated emission** from a population-inverted biological medium: the fighter as gain medium.

### 3.4 Levitation and Flight

Three mechanisms, all bad. Propellant ejection (Tsiolkovsky) requires venting body mass. Geomagnetic coupling (~50 μT) yields Lorentz forces 6–8 orders below body weight. The remaining option is direct stress-energy tensor modification (Alcubierre, 1994) requiring exotic matter — making Saiyans macroscopic violators of the averaged null energy condition.

### 3.5 Conservation

A Kamehameha has no plausible chemical or nuclear source within body mass. The two reservoirs are matter-antimatter annihilation (would require multi-gram antimatter, producing lethal 511 keV gamma signatures) or vacuum/zero-point extraction. As Carroll (2010) emphasizes: the vacuum is not a battery.

## 4. Neuroendocrinology of the Transformation Trigger

### 4.1 The Canonical Trigger

The first documented SSJ transformation (Son Goku, Namek, Age 762) was precipitated by acute emotional trauma. Neuroanatomically: basolateral amygdala (BLA) → central nucleus (CeA) → periaqueductal gray (PAG) dorsolateral column (LeDoux, 2000; Tovote et al., 2016), with locus coeruleus high-tonic discharge flooding cortex with norepinephrine (Aston-Jones & Cohen, 2005). The phenotypic correlates (piloerection, scleral vasoconstriction, hypertensive aura) are consistent with a sympathetic storm of pheochromocytoma magnitude.

### 4.2 Hysterical Strength and the Central Governor

Mothers lifting cars off children represent terrestrial SSJ. Untrained voluntary activation rarely exceeds 30–40% of theoretical maximal force; elite athletes plateau at 90–95% (Enoka & Duchateau, 2008). The remainder is held in reserve by Noakes's **central governor** (2012). Super Saiyan is governor override.

### 4.3 Engineering Chronic Access

**Non-invasive tier:** transcutaneous auricular vagal nerve stimulation (Frangos et al., 2015) plus transcranial focused ultrasound targeting BLA/PAG (Legon et al., 2018).

**Invasive tier:** chemogenetic DREADDs (hM3Dq) in CeA→PAG projection neurons (Roth, 2016); channelrhodopsin-2 in TH+ LC neurons (Carter et al., 2010) for phasic norepinephrine bursts synchronized to combat tempo.

### 4.4 Pharmacological Cocktail

- Modafinil 200 mg — orexinergic wakefulness (Minzenberg & Carter, 2008)
- Selegiline 5 mg — MAO-B inhibition
- Sub-anesthetic ketamine 0.3 mg/kg IV — AMPA potentiation and mTOR-dependent spine growth (Li et al., 2010) — arguably the molecular correlate of "powering up"
- Tianeptine 12.5 mg — buffers stress-induced hippocampal atrophy (McEwen et al., 2010)

*This regimen is not FDA-approved for planetary defense.*

### 4.5 Risks

Exertional rhabdomyolysis with myoglobinuric renal failure; takotsubo cardiomyopathy (Wittstein et al., 2005); serotonin syndrome; frank psychosis. Goku's post-transformation appetite is consistent with massive glycogen depletion; less canonically depicted are the CK levels north of 100,000 U/L.

### 4.6 Flow and Neurotheology

Csikszentmihalyi's (1990) flow state — transient hypofrontality (Dietrich, 2003) — produces performance ceiling elevations without exogenous catecholamines. Newberg's neurotheological imaging (2001) reveals hypoperfusion of the superior parietal lobule, plausible neural correlate of Super Saiyan Blue's "calm in the eye of the storm."

### 4.7 The Training Arc Is Real

Deliberate practice (Ericsson et al., 1993) drives use-dependent myelination (McKenzie et al., 2014), elevates BDNF/TrkB signaling (Cotman & Berchtold, 2002), and produces corticospinal hypertrophy. The 100× gravity chamber is extreme deliberate practice with built-in eccentric loading.

## 5. Structural Materials: Hair, Skin, Bone, Aura

### 5.1 Hair Levitation

Per-strand mass ~2 mg; gravitational load ~2 × 10⁻⁵ N. Keratin (E ≈ 3.5 GPa; McKittrick et al., 2012) is adequate in tension but fails Euler buckling for 20 cm columns. **Solution A:** scalp potential of ~50 kV (Van de Graaff) — costs corona discharge (Paschen breakdown 3 MV/m). **Solution B (preferred):** ferromagnetic keratin via *mamAB* magnetosome operon (Bazylinski & Frankel, 2004; Komeili, 2012) ported into follicular keratinocytes, erected by a 0.5–1 T Halbach array concealed in a high collar (precedent exists in the source material).

### 5.2 Dermal Reinforcement

Human skin UTS ~20 MPa; Kevlar-29 ~3 GPa; monolayer graphene 130 GPa (Lee et al., 2008). A subdermal mesh of CVD graphene laminated with recombinant dragline spider silk from the Lewis transgenic goats (Lewis, 2006; Service, 2002) offers composite UTS in hundreds of MPa.

### 5.3 Skeletal Replacement

Cortical bone yields at ~150 MPa (Currey, 2002); Ti-6Al-4V ~1000 MPa; aligned CNT composites 50–100 GPa (Yu et al., 2000). Pragmatic Saiyan skeleton: CNT-reinforced titanium matrix composite, hollow to preserve marrow, resonance-tuned to avoid sympathetic shattering during the customary screaming.

### 5.4 The Golden Aura

Riboflavin absorbs at 450 nm and emits at ~525 nm with Φ ≈ 0.3 (Koziol, 1966). Subdermal saturation pumped by an endogenous 450 nm bioluminescent layer (engineered from *Aequorea* GFP variants or bacterial *lux* cassettes) produces a tunable yellow-green glow scaling with metabolic state — conveniently, with power level.

### 5.5 The Extremis Shortcut

All of the above can be subsumed into a conformal nanomaterial exosuit optically indistinguishable from skin (Ellis & Granov, 2005). This sidesteps germline modification, ethics review, and the goat farm — at the cost of admitting that the transformation is, structurally, a very good outfit.

## 6. Gravity Chamber and Hyperbolic Time Chamber

### 6.1 The Capsule Corp Gravity Chamber

Bulma's spherical 300–450G chamber is non-compliant with human physiology. Acute tolerance ceiling: ~46G (+Gx), established by Stapp's Sonic Wind No. 1 runs (1954). Chronic tolerances above ~4–5 Gz induce G-LOC within seconds (Burton, 1988). The realized prototype is a 5 m radius enclosed centrifuge gondola (à la DLR Cologne, NASA Ames, IBMP Moscow) at <30 rpm to minimize Coriolis.

### 6.2 Dose-Response

JAXA studies and ground 2G centrifugation show cortical bone density and trabecular thickness increases after 4 weeks (Shiba et al., 2017). Realistic prescription: **2–3G chronic with periodic 5–10G acute pulses of 30–120s**, AI-modulated via closed-loop NIRS, ECG, and ocular pressure monitoring.

### 6.3 Hyperbolic Time Chamber: Real Time Dilation Is Off the Table

The canonical 365:1 ratio demands γ = 365, requiring v ≈ 0.9999962c. Accelerating 80 kg to this velocity costs (γ−1)mc² ≈ 2.6 × 10²¹ J — ~6,000 years of total current global electricity production. The GR alternative requires positioning at r ≈ 1.0000075 r_s of a stellar-mass black hole; tidal forces (~10⁸ G/m) would spaghettify the trainee long before the first kata.

### 6.4 The Subjective-Time Workaround

1. **BCI-driven accelerated VR under pharmacological torpor.** Roth's H₂S-induced suspended animation (Blackstone, Morrison & Roth, 2005) reversibly suppresses metabolism to ~10% basal. Combined with perfluorocarbon liquid ventilation (Clark & Gollan, 1966; Greenspan et al., 1990) and Neuralink-class intracortical arrays, weeks of motor consolidation can compress into days wall-clock, exploiting sleep-dependent memory consolidation (Walker & Stickgold, 2004).

2. **Closed environmental loop.** FiO₂ ≈ 0.12 (~4,500 m altitude) drives HIF-1α-mediated erythropoiesis (EPAS1 Tibetan allele); intermittent FiCO₂ pulses to 3% drive respiratory adaptation; low-dose chronic gamma exposure (10–50 mSv/yr) primes DNA damage response within ICRP limits (radiation hormesis per Calabrese & Baldwin, 2003).

## 7. Endocrine Optimization

### 7.1 Baseline and Supraphysiologic Ceiling

Bhasin et al. (*NEJM* 1996): 600 mg/week testosterone enanthate + resistance training → ~6 kg FFM gain over 10 weeks, triple placebo+exercise. This represents the upper bound of sanctioned enhancement and is several orders of magnitude shy of deflecting a Ki blast.

### 7.2 Conventional AAS Toxicity

Erythrocytosis with thromboembolic risk; concentric LVH and diastolic dysfunction (Baggish et al., *Circulation* 2017); HPTA suppression; 17α-alkylated oral hepatotoxicity. Continuous echocardiography, therapeutic phlebotomy, and hepatic elastography are mandatory.

### 7.3 Next-Generation Modulators

- **SARMs** (RAD-140, LGD-4033): tissue-selective anabolism, attenuated cardiovascular liability
- **Bimagrumab** (anti-ActRII): ~7% lean mass gain in sarcopenic adults (Rooks et al., 2020)
- **Stamulumab** (MYO-029): proof-of-concept in MD (Wagner et al., 2008)
- **Follistatin gene therapy** (AAV-FS344): reversed wasting in primates (Kota et al., *Sci Transl Med* 2009)

### 7.4 The Saiyan Tail

Humans retain coccyx and transient embryonic tail (regressed by Carnegie stage 17), implying silenced developmental modules. Levin lab at Tufts has demonstrated bioelectric limb regrowth in *Xenopus* (Murugan et al., *Sci Adv* 2022); axolotl regeneration uses reactivated Wnt/β-catenin and Shh (Kragl et al., *Nature* 2009). Protocol: localized AAV-*Wnt3a*/*Shh* with bioelectric patterning over sacrococcygeal region.

### 7.5 Catecholamine Reserves

Allogeneic or iPSC-derived chromaffin cell transplants — extending adrenal-to-striatal autograft techniques (Backlund et al., *J Neurosurg* 1985) — provide an expanded epinephrine pool. A "designer adrenal" with tunable PNMT expression permits on-demand surges without chronic hypertensive sequelae.

### 7.6 Thyroid Hyperdrive

Supraphysiologic T3 raises metabolic ceiling. Non-selective β-blockade (propranolol) both blunts adrenergic signaling and inhibits peripheral T4→T3 conversion, providing a useful brake.

### 7.7 Klotho Overexpression

Viral overexpression of α-klotho improves synaptic plasticity and lifespan (Dubal et al., *Cell Rep* 2014). For warriors expecting repeated TBI from intercontinental combat, this is non-optional.

### 7.8 Senzu Bean Pharmacology

Polypharmaceutical cocktail: BPC-157 + TB-500 (tendon/ligament/visceral repair; Sikiric et al., 2010), recombinant IL-10 (cytokine storm resolution), EPO (oxygen-carrying capacity), plerixafor (CXCR4 antagonist — mobilizes HSCs and MSCs; DiPersio et al., *Blood* 2009). Delivered as a chewable lyophilized wafer with faint legume aftertaste.

## 8. Cybernetic Augmentation: The Android-Saiyan Hybrid Path

Cell, Androids 17/18, and post-bisection Frieza demonstrate that silicon is a legitimate shortcut. The Saiyan ceiling is not the universe's ceiling.

### 8.1 Neural I/O — the Internalized Scouter

Bandwidth across the skull bottlenecks all integration. Current platforms: Utah arrays (96–256 ch, penetrating), ECoG meshes (~10⁴ ch, surface), Synchron Stentrode (endovascular via jugular; Oxley et al., 2018), and Neuralink N1 (1,024-ch polymer thread, R1 robotic placement; first human implant Jan 2024).

### 8.2 Motor Unit Recruitment Override

Henneman size principle is metabolically sensible and combat-catastrophic. The Courtine group at EPFL (Wagner et al., *Nature* 2018; Rowald et al., *Nat Med* 2022) restored locomotion in paraplegics via epidural lumbosacral stimulation. Inverting this — supramaximal synchronous recruitment in a healthy cord — is the neuromuscular signature of a Kaio-ken multiplier, at cost of accelerated fatigue and tendon avulsion (Frey Law & Avin, 2010).

### 8.3 Sub-50 ms Reflex Loop

Unaugmented visuomotor latency: 150–250 ms (Welford, 1980). EMG predicts contraction 50–100 ms before mechanical force (Hodges & Bui, 1996). Direct M1 microstimulation bypasses volitional gating with axonal-conduction-bounded latency (~10 ms cortex-to-hand). Stacked: sub-50 ms is conservative.

### 8.4 Sensory Augmentation

Argus II retinal architecture accepts IR-band CMOS front end → thermographic vision useful for spotting ki-suppressed opponents. Subdermal magnetometer + vibrotactile actuator (Cyborg Nest North Sense; Harbisson antenna) is integrated as a genuine sense within weeks (Norimoto & Ikegaya, 2015 rodent analog).

### 8.5 Power

Lithium chemistries cap at ~1 MJ/kg — one afternoon of serious fighting. Pu-238 RTG: ~0.5 W/g thermal for 87 years (Voyager, Curiosity) — sufficient for trickle-charging supercapacitor burst buffers. **Unlimited duty cycle is achievable; unlimited burst power is not** — the cybernetic equivalent of Saiyan stamina depletion.

### 8.6 Skill Transfer

Berger and Song's USC hippocampal memory prosthesis (Hampson et al., *J Neural Eng* 2018) restored *and enhanced* recall via CA3→CA1 MIMO stimulation — the first empirical foothold on Matrix-style upload.

### 8.7 Failure Modes

Glial encapsulation degrades penetrating-electrode SNR over 6–24 months (Polikov et al., 2005). Skin-bridging hardware = permanent MRSA highway. Hermetic seal failure. RTG shielding mass. And the **Argus II failure mode**: your vendor pulls EOL firmware and bricks your retinas. Worst of all: the **Theranos mode** — a charismatic founder promising Cell-tier integration on a Series B timeline, delivering a press release and a federal indictment. *Caveat cyborg.*

## 9. The Saiyan Diet

### 9.1 Caloric Arms Race

Goku's depicted 40,000+ kcal/sitting vs. Michael Phelps' 12,000 kcal/day (Crouse, *NYT*, 2008) and Tour de France mountain stages at 8,000–9,000 kcal/day (Saris et al., 1989) — a 3–5× violation.

### 9.2 Gut as Rate-Limiting Reactor

Carbohydrate uptake caps at ~60 g/hr single-transporter (SGLT1), ~90 g/hr with fructose co-ingestion recruiting GLUT5 (Jeukendrup, *Sports Med* 2014). Mixed-macro absorption tops at ~600 kcal/hr in gut-adapted athletes. 40,000 kcal = 67 hours of continuous eating, or radical restructuring.

### 9.3 Engineering Hyper-Absorption

Lgr5⁺ intestinal stem cells (Barker, Clevers, *Nature* 2007); R-spondin1/Wnt agonism (de Lau et al., *Nature* 2011) induces crypt hyperplasia and villus elongation, increasing SGLT1/GLUT2/PepT1 density. Cost: oncogenic risk — a Saiyan presumably tolerates this.

### 9.4 Bypassing the Lumen

Industrial-scale TPN via implanted Hickman/Broviac with programmable pump. Standard tops at 35 kcal/kg/day (ASPEN, 2016) to avoid refeeding syndrome and hepatic steatosis. Saiyan protocol requires novel high-osmolarity formulations with aggressive insulin co-infusion.

### 9.5 Substrate Flexibility

FASTER study (Volek et al., *Metabolism* 2016): keto-adapted athletes oxidize fat at ~1.5 g/min — 2.3× high-carb controls. Combine keto-adaptation + MCT + exogenous ketone esters (Cox et al., *Cell Metab* 2016): three parallel ATP streams. Randle cycle competition sidestepped by sheer mitochondrial density.

### 9.6 Supplementation Stack

- **Creatine monohydrate** 5g/d (Kreider et al., 2017) — most validated ergogenic aid
- **Leucine/HMB** — leucine triggers mTORC1 via Sestrin2 displacement (Wolfson et al., *Science* 2016)
- **Hydrolyzed collagen + vitamin C** 15g pre-training doubles collagen synthesis (Shaw et al., *AJCN* 2017)
- **Glycine** 3g pre-sleep (Yamadera et al., 2007)

### 9.7 Microbiome Engineering

Scheiman et al. (*Nat Med* 2019) isolated *Veillonella atypica* from Boston Marathon runners — lactate-metabolizing gut bacteria enhance treadmill endurance in mice. *Akkermansia muciniphila* improves gut barrier (Depommier et al., 2019). Protocol: FMT from curated elite-athlete donor pool — a microbial draft pick — with periodic *Veillonella*/*Akkermansia* boosters.

### 9.8 Senzu Bean v1.0

8 g, ~400 kcal: highly branched cluster dextrin for rapid gastric emptying; 20g whey hydrolysate; 5g MCT; liposomal-encapsulated A/D/E/K and B-complex; 500 mg exogenous ATP disodium (PEAK ATP; Jäger et al., 2014); creatine; citrulline malate; *Veillonella* probiotic capsule. Not regenerative — but metabolically the closest extant analogue.

## 10. Ethics, Regulation, Jurisdiction, and Capital Allocation

### 10.1 The Billionaire-Subject Loophole

The Common Rule (45 CFR 46) applies to "research" with "human subjects" under federal funding or FDA submission. Privately funded n=1 self-experimentation outside an IND is, strictly, not IRB-jurisdictional. Precedents: Barry Marshall (*H. pylori*, 1984), Josiah Zayner (DIY CRISPR myostatin, 2017), Bryan Johnson (Project Blueprint). **Caveats:** clinicians remain bound by state licensure; cross-state investigational drug movement triggers FDA jurisdiction under 21 USC §355; Helsinki §4 binds physicians regardless of funding. The loophole is real but narrower than Twitter suggests.

### 10.2 The Germline Wall

Somatic interventions follow conventional FDA/EMA/NMPA pathways. The wall appears at heritable germline modification. Oviedo Convention Art. 13 (1997) prohibits. US Dickey-Wicker (1996, annually renewed) bars federal funding. FDA appropriations language since 2015 prohibits acceptance of heritable INDs. China criminalized unauthorized germline editing in 2020 post-He Jiankui. **He Jiankui: 3-year prison sentence, ¥3M fine.** Expect this as the floor, not ceiling.
